Account Deletion Request

At HTCMX Tabere, we value your privacy. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data (name, email, phone number, booking history) at any time.

What happens when you delete your account?

  • All your personal profile information will be permanently removed.
  • Your booking history will be anonymized or deleted.
  • This action is irreversible.

How to request deletion:

To initiate the deletion process, please send an email from the address associated with your account to:

francisc.bblasca@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Our team will process your request within 48 hours and confirm once the data has been removed.
