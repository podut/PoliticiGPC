# Privacy Policy for PawCare – Vet & Pet Care Log (Medforpets)

**Last Updated: April 19, 2026**

At PawCare (Medforpets), we value the privacy of our users and their pets. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("the App"), which is designed as a personal log and informational tool for pet owners.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Camera and Image Data
The App requires access to your device's **Camera**. 
- **Usage:** Used exclusively for the "AI Pet Assistant" feature to help identify common pet-related issues (such as fleas or skin irritations) or to organize pet care notes.
- **Processing:** Images are transmitted securely to the **Google Gemini API** for real-time informational analysis. 
- **Storage:** PawCare **does not store** your images on our servers. Images are processed in-memory and discarded after the analysis is complete.

### B. Location Data
The App requires access to your **Location** (Fine and Coarse location).
- **Usage:** Used to provide the "Veterinarians Nearby" feature, helping you find local clinics on a map based on your current position.
- **Control:** You can enable or disable location services at any time through your device settings.

### C. Local Pet Records
Information you enter about your pets (name, breed, age, weight, care history) is stored **locally on your device** using an internal database.
- We do not have access to this data. It remains on your device for your personal use.

### D. Analytics and Performance
We use basic analytics (e.g., Google Analytics for Firebase) to monitor App performance and crashes. This data is anonymized.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

PawCare utilizes the following third-party services:
- **Google Play Services:** For App distribution and core functionality.
- **Google Gemini API:** For AI-powered pet care informational analysis.
- **Google Maps API:** For displaying nearby pet-related locations.

---

## 3. Data Retention and Security

- **Security:** We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transit.
- **Retention:** Most data is stored locally. AI-processed images are not retained.

---

## 4. Important Disclaimers

- **Non-Medical App:** PawCare is a **Pet Care Log** and informational tool. It is NOT a medical app and is not intended for human health research or medical use.
- **No Veterinary Advice:** The AI analysis is for informational purposes only. It DOES NOT constitute professional veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for health-related concerns regarding your pets.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for pet owners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

---

## 6. User Rights

Since PawCare stores most data locally, you can manage your data by deleting pet profiles within the App or uninstalling the App.
