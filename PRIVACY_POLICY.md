# Privacy Policy for Medforpets

**Last Updated: April 12, 2026**

At Medforpets, we value the privacy of our users and their pets. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Camera and Image Data
The App requires access to your device's **Camera**. 
- **Usage:** Used exclusively for the "AI Scan" feature to identify potential health issues (fleas, irritations, etc.) or to scan medication leaflets.
- **Processing:** Images taken are transmitted securely to the **Google Gemini API** for real-time analysis. 
- **Storage:** Medforpets **does not store** your images on our servers. Images are processed in-memory and discarded after the analysis is complete.

### B. Location Data
The App requires access to your **Location** (Fine and Coarse location).
- **Usage:** Used to provide the "Veterinarians Nearby" feature, allowing you to find clinics on a map based on your current position.
- **Control:** You can enable or disable location services at any time through your device settings.

### C. Local Pet Data
Information you enter about your pets (name, breed, age, weight, medical history) is stored **locally on your device** using an internal database (Room DB).
- We do not have access to this data unless you explicitly use a cloud-sync feature (if provided in future updates).

### D. Analytics and Performance
We use basic analytics (e.g., Google Analytics for Firebase) to monitor App performance and crashes. This data is anonymized and does not identify you personally.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

Medforpets utilizes the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own policies:
- **Google Play Services:** For App distribution and core functionality.
- **Google Gemini API:** For AI-powered medical analysis.
- **Google Maps API:** For displaying veterinarian clinics.

---

## 3. Data Retention and Security

- **Security:** We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
- **Retention:** Since most data is stored locally, it remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its cache.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for pet owners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

---

## 5. User Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access the data we have about you.
- Request the deletion of your data.
- Opt-out of data collection.

Since Medforpets stores most data locally, you can exercise these rights by simply deleting the pet profiles within the App or uninstalling the App.

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
