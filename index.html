Privacy Policy and Terms for PawCare

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

1. Medical Disclaimer (IMPORTANT)

PawCare is NOT a medical app and is not intended for human use.

  • Non-Medical Purpose: This app is a personal journal and informational tool dedicated exclusively to pet owners. It is NOT intended for the diagnosis, cure, treatment, or prevention of any disease or condition in humans or animals.
  • No Veterinary Advice: The information provided by the "AI Wellness Assistant" is for guidance only. It DOES NOT replace a consultation, diagnosis, or treatment provided by a licensed veterinarian.
  • Emergencies: In case of a pet emergency, contact a veterinary clinic immediately.

2. Information We Collect

A. Camera and Images (Permissions)

  • Used exclusively for the "AI Pet Assistant" feature (identifying common pet-related issues such as fleas or irritations).
  • Processing: Images are transmitted securely to the Google Gemini API for real-time analysis.
  • Retention: PawCare DOES NOT store images on its servers. Processing happens in-memory, and data is deleted immediately after analysis.

B. Location Data

  • Used exclusively to display nearby veterinary clinics on a map. Users can revoke access at any time through their device settings.

C. Local Records

  • All pet-related data (name, age, activity history) is stored locally on your device. The developer does not have access to this data.

3. Third-Party Services

The app utilizes the following Google services:

  • Google Play Services (System functionality)
  • Google Gemini API (Informational AI analysis)
  • Google Maps API (Map display)

4. Data Security

All necessary data transmissions (to the AI API) are encrypted using standard protocols (TLS/SSL).
