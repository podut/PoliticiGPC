Privacy Policy and Terms for HTCMX Tabere

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

1. General Terms

HTCMX Tabere is an application dedicated to airsoft events and outdoor activities management.

  • Purpose: This app allows users to book spots in airsoft events and browse related equipment.
  • Responsibility: Participation in events is voluntary and follows the safety rules of the organizer.

2. Information We Collect

A. Account and Registration

  • We collect name, email, and phone number for event booking and account management.
  • Storage: Data is stored securely on our MongoDB servers hosted via Coolify.

B. Camera and Images

  • The app may request camera access to allow administrators to upload product or event images.

C. Notifications

  • We use notifications to send booking confirmations and event updates.

3. Third-Party Services

The app utilizes the following services:

  • Google Play Services (Core functionality)
  • Stripe (Payment processing)
  • Coil (Image loading)

4. Data Deletion

Users can request the deletion of their account and all associated data at any time.

To initiate a deletion request, please visit our dedicated page: Account Deletion Request

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) to protect your data during transmission.
